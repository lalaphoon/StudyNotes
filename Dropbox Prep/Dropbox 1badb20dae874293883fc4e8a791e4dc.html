General Background of Dropbox

  • What is Dropbox?

    https://www.businessinsider.com/what-is-dropbox

    • What is it?
      • Dropbox is a cloud storage service that lets you save files online and sync them to your devices.
      • You can use Dropbox links to share files and folders with other people without sending large attachments. 
      • Dropbox offers a free plan that includes 2 GB of storage. You can pay to increase that to as much as 3 TB with a subscription.
    • How?
      • Desktop and web
      • synchronizing your Dropbox files locally and online
    • Key features
      • multiplatforms - desktop, mobile, web browser, Windows, Mac, iOS, Android
      • 256-bit AES Advanced Encryption Standard
      • Level of permission : share files and foleders using specific link
      • Use link for email attachment
      • history of versioning, 30 days of file recovery
  • What is dropbox doing
    • Dropbox is the world’s first smart workspace that helps people and teams to work cooperatively.
    • User Amount: 600 million registered users across 180 countries
